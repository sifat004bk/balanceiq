# BalanceIQ - Fundraising Strategy

**Market:** Bangladesh
**Version:** 1.0 BD
**Date:** 2025-11-16

---

## How Much Funding Needed

### Seed Round: 60 Lakh BDT (~$50,000 USD)

**Use:** MVP development + 6-month operations + initial user acquisition

**Runway:** 12 months (conservative burn rate)

**Dilution:** 15-20% equity

---

## What Will the Funding Be Used For

### Breakdown: 60 Lakh BDT Total

**1. Product Development (25 Lakh BDT = 42%)**

**Team (6 months):**
- 1 Full-stack Developer: 60,000 BDT/month × 6 = 3.6L
- 1 Mobile App Developer (Flutter): 50,000 BDT/month × 6 = 3L
- 1 AI/ML Engineer (part-time/freelance): 40,000 BDT/month × 6 = 2.4L
- 1 UI/UX Designer (contract): 30,000 BDT/month × 3 = 0.9L
- **Subtotal:** 9.9L BDT

**Infrastructure:**
- Cloud hosting (AWS/GCP): 15,000 BDT/month × 12 = 1.8L
- AI API costs (OpenAI/Anthropic): 10,000 BDT/month × 12 = 1.2L
- Development tools & licenses: 0.5L
- **Subtotal:** 3.5L BDT

**External Services:**
- bKash/Nagad payment gateway integration: 1L
- OCR/Speech APIs: 0.5L
- **Subtotal:** 1.5L

**Bangla Localization:**
- Translation (UI + AI responses): 0.3L
- Bangla voice testing: 0.2L
- **Subtotal:** 0.5L

**Development Buffer (unexpected costs):** 2.0L

**Total Development:** 17.4L + 7.6L reserve = 25L

---

**2. User Acquisition & Marketing (20 Lakh BDT = 33%)**

**First 6 Months (Launch + Growth):**
- Facebook/Instagram ads: 8L (50,000 BDT/month avg, scaled)
- YouTube influencer partnerships: 3L (5-6 micro-influencers)
- Content creation (videos, graphics): 2L
- App Store Optimization (ASO): 0.5L
- PR & media outreach: 1L
- Community building (events, challenges): 1.5L
- Referral program incentives: 2L
- Campus ambassador program: 2L (10 students × 3 months × 3,000 BDT + coordination)

**Total Marketing:** 20L

---

**3. Operations & Overhead (10 Lakh BDT = 17%)**

**6-Month Operations:**
- Office space (co-working): 15,000 BDT/month × 6 = 0.9L
- Internet, utilities, misc: 5,000 BDT/month × 6 = 0.3L
- Legal & compliance: 1.5L
  - Company registration: 0.3L
  - Privacy policy, terms: 0.2L
  - Payment gateway contracts: 0.5L
  - Buffer: 0.5L
- Accounting & bookkeeping: 10,000 BDT/month × 6 = 0.6L
- Customer support tools (Zendesk/Intercom): 0.2L
- Insurance & contingency: 1L

**Total Operations:** 4.5L

**Founder/CEO Salary (Minimal):**
- 40,000 BDT/month × 6 months = 2.4L
- **Founders work at below-market rates to extend runway**

**Operations Buffer:** 3.1L

**Total Operations:** 10L

---

**4. Reserve & Contingency (5 Lakh BDT = 8%)**

- Unexpected technical challenges: 2L
- Market testing/pivots: 1.5L
- Emergency fund: 1.5L

**Total Reserve:** 5L

## Funding Sources for Bangladesh Startup

### Option 1: Angel Investors (Bangladesh)

**Target Amount:** 30-40 Lakh BDT ($25K-35K)

**Who to Approach:**

**Bangladesh Angel Network:**
- Active angel investors in Dhaka
- Ticket size: 10-50 Lakh BDT
- Focus: Tech startups, fintech

**Individual Angels:**
- Successful entrepreneurs who exited (Chaldal, Pathao, Shohoz founders/early employees)
- Corporate executives (Grameenphone, bKash, Daraz leadership)
- NRB (Non-Resident Bangladeshi) tech professionals in US/UK

**Where to Find Them:**
- Dhaka tech events (Startup Dhaka, BASIS events)
- LinkedIn (search "angel investor Bangladesh")
- Personal network introductions

**Pitch Angle:**
- "Bangladesh-first fintech solving local problem"
- "Low CAC market, 10x cheaper than US"
- "Path to profitability in 18-24 months"
- "Exit via acquisition by bKash, Nagad, or regional expansion"

---

### Option 2: Venture Capital (Local BD Funds)

**Target Amount:** 40-60 Lakh BDT ($35K-50K)

**Bangladesh VC Landscape:**

**Venture Capital Funds:**
1. **LightCastle Partners** - Seed/early stage, fintech focus
2. **Anchorless Bangladesh** - Early stage tech
3. **SAJIDA Foundation** - Impact + fintech
4. **Accelerating Asia** - Regional but invests in BD
5. **IDLC VC Fund** - Emerging BD fund

**Typical Terms:**
- Equity: 15-25% for seed round
- Board seat likely
- Milestone-based disbursement

**Requirements:**
- MVP or beta product
- 500-1,000 users (traction proof)
- Clear path to revenue
- Founding team credibility

**Timeline:** 3-6 months from first contact to funds in bank

---

### Option 3: Accelerators

**Target Amount:** 10-25 Lakh BDT + Mentorship

**Bangladesh Accelerators:**

1. **Grameenphone Accelerator**
   - Investment: 10-15 Lakh BDT
   - Equity: 8-10%
   - Duration: 3-4 months
   - Best for: Mobile-first startups
   - Benefit: Access to GP customer base (80M users)

2. **bKash Accelerator** (if exists/launches)
   - Perfect fit for our use case
   - Integration support
   - Marketing via bKash app

3. **Bangladesh Angels Network (BAN) Accelerator**
   - Cohort-based program
   - Demo day to angel investors
   - Less cash, more mentorship

**International Accelerators (Remote):**

4. **Y Combinator** (US)
   - $500K for 7% (unlikely for BD pre-traction startup)
   - Remote batch possible
   - Brand value immense

5. **Antler** (Regional - operates in SEA)
   - Invests pre-idea to seed
   - $100K-200K typical

**Recommendation:** Start with local (Grameenphone) to validate, then international if traction strong.

---

### Option 4: Grants & Competitions

**Non-Dilutive Funding (Free Money):**

**Startup Competitions:**
1. **Startup Bangladesh (Govt Program)**
   - Grant: 5-15 Lakh BDT
   - No equity taken
   - Application process, pitch competition

2. **BASIS National ICT Awards**
   - Recognition + prize money (2-5 Lakh)
   - PR value high

3. **Global Competitions (Remote Entry):**
   - Seedstars (pitch in Dhaka, global finals)
   - Google for Startups (product credits)
   - AWS Activate (cloud credits worth $10K-100K)

**Corporate Innovation Challenges:**
- bKash Innovation Challenge (if announced)
- Grameenphone Tech Challenge
- IDLC Innovation Fund

**Total Potential from Grants/Competitions:** 10-20 Lakh BDT (non-dilutive)

---

### Option 5: Bootstrapping (Self-Funded)

**If You Have Personal Savings:**

**Minimum Viable Budget: 15-20 Lakh BDT ($12K-17K)**

**Lean Approach:**
- 2 co-founders (you + 1 technical) working full-time, no salary
- Freelance developers for specific tasks (10-15L)
- No office (work from home)
- Organic marketing only (0-2L)
- 6-month runway to MVP + 500 beta users

**Pros:**
- No dilution
- Full control
- Proof of commitment to future investors

**Cons:**
- Slow growth
- Personal financial risk
- Hard to scale without capital

**Recommendation:** Bootstrap to MVP → raise seed with traction

## Investor Pitch Summary (3-4 Key Points)

**Use This Pitch (60-Second Version):**

"BalanceIQ is a chat-based AI expense tracker built for Bangladesh's cash and mobile money economy.

**The Problem:** 40 million middle-class Bangladeshis use bKash and Nagad daily but have no way to track where their money goes. They resort to Excel or notebooks - tedious and often abandoned.

**Our Solution:** Chat with our AI to log expenses in seconds. Voice messages while commuting. Photo receipts at restaurants. Works in Bangla and English. No bank account needed.

**Market Opportunity:** Bangladesh-first strategy means 10x lower customer acquisition costs than US ($2-5 vs $30-60). We can reach profitability with 10,000 paying users at 600 BDT/month.

**Traction:** [Insert current numbers: 500 beta users, 4.5-star rating, 100 daily active users]

**Ask:** We're raising 60 Lakh BDT for 15-20% equity to scale to 10,000 users and 30L BDT monthly revenue within 12 months."

## Timeline for Fundraising

### Month 1-2: Preparation
- Finalize pitch deck (10 slides max)
- Create 1-minute demo video
- Financial model spreadsheet (3-year projections)
- Build MVP or clickable prototype
- Get 50-100 beta users (proof of concept)

### Month 3-4: Outreach
- Identify 20 potential investors (angels + VCs + accelerators)
- Warm introductions via network
- Apply to accelerators (Grameenphone, BAN)
- Cold outreach on LinkedIn

### Month 5-6: Pitching
- 10-15 pitch meetings
- Iterate pitch based on feedback
- Handle due diligence requests
- Negotiate terms

### Month 7: Closing
- Term sheet from 1-2 investors
- Legal paperwork (shareholder agreement)
- Funds received

**Total Timeline: 6-7 months from decision to raise → money in bank**

**Faster Path:** Accelerator application (3-4 months if accepted)

## Fallback Plan if Can't Raise

### Plan B: Lean Bootstrap (20 Lakh BDT Personal Investment)

**What This Looks Like:**

**Team:**
- 2 co-founders (no salary for 6 months)
- 1 contract developer: 40K × 6 months = 2.4L
- 1 designer (one-time): 0.5L

**Product:**
- MVP only: Text logging + basic dashboard
- Skip voice, photo OCR (add later)
- English-only initially (Bangla Phase 2)

**Marketing:**
- 100% organic: Facebook groups, campus ambassadors, referrals
- Budget: 2-3L for 6 months

**Timeline:**
- Months 1-3: Build MVP
- Months 4-6: Launch, get 1,000 users organically
- Month 7: Raise seed round with traction ($50K easier to raise with proof)

**Revenue Target:**
- 100 paying users × 600 BDT = 60K/month by Month 6
- Covers server costs, enables hiring

---

### Plan C: Service Business Hybrid

**If Product Doesn't Work:**

**Pivot to B2B:**
- Sell "AI Expense Tracking" as a service to companies
- Corporate expense management for 20-50 employee companies
- Price: 50,000-100,000 BDT/month per company (10-20 employees)

**Revenue:**
- 5 clients × 75K/month = 3.75L/month revenue
- Funds continued product development for B2C

**Use Learnings:**
- Build better product while earning
- Profitable without venture capital
- Grow B2C when timing is right

---

### Plan D: Acqui-hire Exit

**If Can't Scale:**

**Partners Who Might Acquire:**
- bKash (build in-house expense tracker)
- Nagad (add feature to their app)
- City Bank / Dutch-Bangla Bank (digital banking feature)

**Value:**
- Technology + team
- Price: 50L-1Cr BDT
- Team joins as employees

**Not failure:** Successful pivot, team gets exit, can start next venture

## Key Milestones That Unlock Funding

**To Raise 60L Seed, You Need:**

**Minimum (Angel Investors):**
- Working MVP (can demo in meeting)
- 500 beta users
- 10% conversion to paid (50 paying users)
- 4.0+ star rating
- Clear founder vision

**Ideal (VC Funding):**
- 2,000-5,000 users
- 200-500 paying users
- $2K-5K MRR (2.5-6L BDT/month)
- 20-30% MoM growth
- Press coverage (1-2 articles)

**Don't Try to Raise If:**
- No product (just idea)
- Zero users
- Team incomplete (solo founder red flag)
- No competitive advantage (anyone can copy)

**Build First, Raise Second.**

## Recommended Approach

**Phase 1: Bootstrap to Validation (3 months, 10-15 Lakh personal)**
- Build MVP
- Get 500-1,000 users
- Prove 8-10% conversion
- Cost: Your time + 10-15L BDT

**Phase 2: Raise Seed (Month 4-6, targeting 60L)**
- With traction, easier to raise
- Better terms (less dilution)
- Approach angels + VCs simultaneously

**Phase 3: Scale with Seed Capital (Month 7-18)**
- Aggressive user acquisition
- Hire 3-5 team members
- Reach 10K users, 1K paying

**Phase 4: Series A / Profitability (Month 18-24)**
- Either raise 5-10Cr BDT Series A to scale regionally
- OR achieve profitability at 10K paying users and grow organically

**Exit Options (Year 3-5):**
- Acquisition by bKash/Nagad/Bank: 10-50Cr BDT
- Regional expansion (India, Pakistan): Raise Series B
- Sustainable profitable business: Run indefinitely
